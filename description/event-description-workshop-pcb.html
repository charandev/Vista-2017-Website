P C B Design Workshop


The Workshop is dedicated in training the students on the design and manufacturing of a Printed Circuit Board. This workshop is an invaluable resource for those who are learning PCB design. Designing a PCB is not something you will do in a couple of hours. It is a highly technical learned skill that will take years to master. The Workshop will provide the basics of PCB design as well as more advanced topics.
